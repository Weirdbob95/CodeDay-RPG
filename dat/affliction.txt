REACH
Option: Extra Condition | 1 | true | 1 | Your Affliction imposes two conditions instead of one at each degree of effect. +1 cost per rank.
Option: Limited Degree | -1 | true | 2 | Your Affliction is limited to no more than two degrees of effect. If you take this option twice, your Affliction is limited to no more than one degree of effect. -1 cost per rank.
Option: Progressive | 2 | true | 1 | Whenever your target fails a saving throw against your Affliction, it increases in effect by one degree. +2 cost per rank.

You can impose debilitating conditions on your enemies. Choose which conditions you impose and which saving throw enemies use to resist this power.

When you use this power, choose a target you can reach to make a saving throw with DC equal to (10 + your Affliction rank). On a failure, your target suffers one of the following conditions: blinded, dazed, deafened, a level of exhaustion, grappled, poisoned, or prone.

If your target fails the saving throw by 5 or more, they instead suffer one of the following conditions: charmed, compelled, frightened, incapacitated, or restrained.

If your target fails the saving throw by 10 or more, they instead suffer one of the following conditions: controlled, paralyzed, petrified, stunned, or unconscious.

Your target can make a saving throw at the ends of each of its turns to remove first and second degree conditions. Third degree conditions heal after a minute outside of combat.
